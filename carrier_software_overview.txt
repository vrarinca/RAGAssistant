Carrier Software is a specialized provider of transportation and insurance analytics solutions that support motor carriers, insurance carriers, brokers, risk managers, and shippers. Founded in 2000, the company offers a suite of data analysis tools designed to meet the evolving needs of the commercial transportation industry by helping users improve safety scores, grow business, and make data‑driven decisions. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

### Company Background
Carrier Software was originally established to provide motor carrier safety data and risk analysis to the largest fleets in the United States. Over time, it expanded its portfolio to include marketing leads, underwriting tools, and fleet monitoring solutions. Core components of the company’s software offerings include safety performance visualization, underwriting reports, and automated client monitoring. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

### Key Solutions and Products
The company’s products can be categorized into several major areas:

1. **Leads and Marketing Tools**

2. **Underwriting Reports**

### Technical and Industry Terms
- **FMCSA**: Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration, the U.S. agency that regulates commercial motor vehicle safety and provides public carrier data. This data is a core input for safety analysis and monitoring tools. :contentReference[oaicite:7]{index=7}  
- **BASIC Scores**: A set of metrics used by FMCSA to assess carrier compliance in areas such as crash rates, unsafe driving, and hours‑of‑service violations. Software tools help visualize and interpret these scores. :contentReference[oaicite:8]{index=8}  
- **Telematics**: The use of telecommunications and information processing to send, receive, and store data related to vehicles and drivers, often used to monitor fleet performance and alerts. :contentReference[oaicite:9]{index=9}
